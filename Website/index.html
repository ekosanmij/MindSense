FAQ

What teams and users ask most

Is MindSense AI making clinical diagnoses?

No. MindSense is a wellness-support product focused on regulation guidance and learning loops.

What is the first-screen outcome?

Within 30 seconds, users should understand their current state and one clear next action.

What makes recommendations change over time?

Recommendation confidence adjusts using outcomes, context fit, and data-quality signals.

How does this handle sparse or inconsistent signals?

MindSense labels confidence and can switch to estimated mode with clear diagnostics and hints.
